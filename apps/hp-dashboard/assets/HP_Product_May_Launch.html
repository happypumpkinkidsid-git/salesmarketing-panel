Product StoryCerita Produk
Your kid doesn't stop — and neither should their clothes. ActiveKnit was born from one problem: kids who want to look good shouldn't have to feel restricted. The polo-style silhouette keeps them looking sharp for class photos and every playtime in between. But underneath that clean look is a stretch fabric engineered for full range of motion — bending, running, spinning — without any tug or pull. 100% high-grade cotton manages sweat and regulates body temperature, so overheated, cranky afternoons become far less frequent. Tennis-inspired polo for boys. Sport skirt for girls. Stylish for the photo. Built for everything after. Anak kamu nggak pernah berhenti — bajunya juga harusnya begitu. ActiveKnit lahir dari satu masalah: anak yang mau tampil bagus seharusnya tidak perlu merasa terkekang. Di balik tampilan bersih itu ada kain stretch untuk gerak tanpa batas. Inti 100% katun mengelola keringat dan mengatur suhu tubuh. Stylish untuk foto. Diciptakan untuk segalanya setelahnya.

Made using Shima Seiki seamless technology, the same Japanese engineering trusted by top-tier fashion houses globally, each PureKnit piece is formed as one complete structure from a single continuous thread. Zero seams. Zero stitches. Zero irritation points. This is not assembly — it is formation. 100% pure cotton — no polyester, no nylon filler — means every thread works for comfort and breathability. The collared silhouette elevates to smart-casual without effort. European editorial in character. Japanese in craft. Indonesian in heart. A first in Indonesian kidswear. Ada baju yang langsung terasa berbeda saat kamu menyentuhnya. PureKnit by Happy Pumpkin adalah perbedaan itu. Dibuat menggunakan teknologi seamless Shima Seiki — rekayasa Jepang yang sama yang dipercaya rumah mode kelas atas di seluruh dunia — setiap produk PureKnit dibentuk sebagai satu struktur utuh dari satu benang yang tidak terputus.
